Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this for?

High‑school students competing in DECA roleplay events who want structured, competitive preparation. We work with students at all levels, from first-time competitors to international finalists looking to win GLASS.

Do you coach beginners?

Absolutely! We build fundamentals first — structure, voice, pacing — then layer advanced frameworks. Every student starts at their own level and follows a personalized improvement path.

Is coaching virtual?

Yes. All sessions run via Zoom with shared timers, prompts, and live feedback. This allows for efficient practice time, screen sharing for visual aids, and real-time recording for self-review.

How does pricing work?

Pricing depends on the program intensity and schedule. We offer packages ranging from one-time competition prep to ongoing weekly sessions. Book a free intro call to discuss your specific needs and get detailed pricing options.

How long are sessions?

Standard sessions are 60 minutes, with extended 90-minute options available for advanced practice. Each session includes multiple roleplay repetitions, structured feedback, and targeted skill development.

What events do you cover?

We specialize in all DECA roleplay categories including Principles, Series, and Team Decision Making events. Our frameworks are adaptable across business management, marketing, finance, hospitality, and entrepreneurship.
